Trips I've been on...

from most recent to oldest

Maine - Acadia National Park

In 2022 I went to Maine with my boyfriend to visit his grandparents. While we were there we went to Acadia National Park and hiked. We also enjoyed shopping, eating good food, reading, and fishing!

Brazil - Porto Alegre

In 2018 I had the opportunity to go to Brazil on a mission trip. I went with a group called Global Advance and we focused on helping in a womens retreat as well as working in the Favelas. On tis trip I had the opportunity to lead worship in Portuguese as well as share two songs I wrote.

Italy - Rome, Venice, and Florence

In 2018 I went to Italy with my family. My brother had a terminal disease and Make-A-Wish sponsered us. We ate lots of homemade pasta, swam in beautiful oceans, walked around famous grounds, and saw masterpeices. I plan to go back to Italy one day to visit Tuscany and live the dream of being on a vinyard in Italy.

New York City

In 2017 my family and I went to New York City. We went after Christmas and ended up getting stuck in freezing weather. It just happened to be one of the coldest winters in in the past few years. However, we did manage to find some of my favorite restaurants in the United States. The list includes: Jacob's Pickle, Carmine's Italian Restaurant, and Tao.
